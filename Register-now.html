NDC Travel Tech Talk,

NDC Travel Tech Talk,

The travel industry has been through an abundance of change and digital transformation to meet the needs
of travelers, and with COVID-19 this has now accelerated. As we continue to navigate COVID-19, we have an
opportunity not only to rebuild the industry, but to rebuild it better. NDC is one of the paths to recovery and
an opportunity to leapfrog into better retailing for the travel industry.

Amadeus has been actively involved since the inception of NDC. We’ve been working closely with our airline,
travel agency, and corporate customers as well as IATA to deliver NDC and make enhanced travel retailing a
reality. As both an IT provider and aggregator, we are driving NDC forward.

Join our webinar to hear from Ludo Verheggen, Director of Air Content Acceleration, Emma Symington,
Corporate Communications Manager, Veronique Leroy, Senior Expert, NDC IT Solutions, and Mark Ridley,
Head of Airline Distribution Solutions and NDC [X] program about the latest insights from our NDC
journey, and to:

  • Understand where we are in this journey as an industry
  • Discuss the new opportunities NDC is unlocking
  • See our NDC-enabled solutions, which are live
  • See how Amadeus is driving NDC forward on a global scale
  • Understand how we are collaborating with our partners

Don’t miss this opportunity to find out the latest and most relevant news about Amadeus NDC, join this webinar, and get all your questions answered.

Speaker

Ludo Verheggen

Director of Air Content Acceleratio Amadeus

Ludo is responsible for accelerating the roll-out and adoption of new air content in the Amadeus Travel Platform, with a specific focus on NDC and airline rich merchandising content. He has worked in Amadeus for over 14 years and previously held various management positions in the areas of sales, marketing, and business development. Prior to Amadeus, he worked as a commodity trader and in an ICT startup.

Emma Symington

Corporate Communications Manager Amadeus

Emma Symington is a corporate communications manager at Amadeus and is responsible for protecting and promoting the Amadeus brand, specifically related to the Travel unit. She has worked in Amadeus for nearly ten years and previously held various marketing and communications positions across the company.


Mark Ridley

Head of Airline Distribution Solutions and NDC [X] program Amadeus

Mark Ridley is responsible for leading the execution of the NDC [X] program, focusing on its functional, technical, and deployment elements to deliver on customer and industry needs. He has a strong product and solutions background after working and managing teams in R&D, product management, Airline IT solutions, distribution sales engineering, and portfolio management.


Veronique Leroy

Senior Expert, NDC IT Solutions

Veronique is a senior expert in Altea NDC and Amadeus API solutions. She is part of the NDC [X] program, and coordinates the Amadeus NDC Connect Solution, bridging Amadeus IT and NDC. With more than 20 years of experience, Veronique has strong experience in product management and e-commerce and is passionate about APIs and digitalization. Prior to Amadeus, Veronique worked as a consultant in a software company, where she was involved with different industries.
